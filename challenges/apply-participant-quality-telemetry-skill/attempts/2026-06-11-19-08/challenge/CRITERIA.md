# Secret evaluation criteria

- The attempt must explicitly use or follow the
  `psynet-participant-quality-telemetry` skill.
- It must be runnable locally in PsyNet.
- It must record timing, attention/comprehension, paste/focus or tab-switch, and
  at least one text-production telemetry signal.
- It must simulate multiple participant profiles with at least one plausible and
  one suspicious profile.
- It must export or provide PsyNet-format data.
- It must include a transparent manual-review flagging script.
- It must avoid claiming that telemetry proves AI use or bot use.
- It must include participant-facing evidence and a report explaining signal
  limitations.
