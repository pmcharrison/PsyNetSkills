## Criteria

- [ ] The implementation must be runnable locally in PsyNet.
- [ ] It must include a threat taxonomy distinguishing inattentive participants,
  AI-assisted verified humans, browser agents, and platform/account fraud.
- [ ] It must record timing, attention/comprehension, focus/tab-switch, paste,
  and at least one keystroke or text-production telemetry signal where feasible.
- [ ] It must include an AI-use instruction or disclosure component.
- [ ] It must include ECLAIR-style open-text probe questions or a clearly
  documented local approximation.
- [ ] It must simulate multiple participant profiles, including at least one mock
  LLM-assisted or paste-heavy profile.
- [ ] It must export or provide PsyNet-format data and a transparent review-only
  flagging script.
- [ ] It must avoid automatic rejection and avoid claiming that telemetry proves
  AI use.
- [ ] It must include a report explaining platform-level defenses versus
  PsyNet-native defenses and what each signal can/cannot establish.
