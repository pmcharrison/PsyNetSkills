# Criteria

- The experiment runs as a PsyNet experiment.
- Participants can make a cooperate-or-defect choice on every round.
- The partner behavior is implemented consistently with the documented partner
  description.
- The payoff matrix is applied correctly for all four possible outcome pairs.
- Feedback shown to participants matches the saved round data.
- Saved data is sufficient to reconstruct the sequence of choices, round payoffs,
  cumulative score, and partner behavior.
- The participant experience explains the game without assuming prior knowledge
  of the Prisoner's Dilemma.
